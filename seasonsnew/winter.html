Memory from Gabby

My favorite winter memory is going to my grandma’s house for Christmas. All of my family goes to her house and we have dinner and open presents. Later, my cousins and I go sledding or snowboarding down the hill in my grandma’s backyard. After sledding we go inside and watch TV.

Tyler Walden
