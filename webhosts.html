Web Hosts Review

Host Gator

Host gator has a free 1 year domain name. Also plans that are only $17 a year. This is a lot cheaper then I was expecting something like this to cost. It has 3 plans. The hatchling plan is $1.42 a month. You get a single website, free domain, and free SSL certificate. The baby plan has unlimited websites. It is $3.50 a month. The business plan has unlimited websites, free dedicated IP, and free domains.

GoDaddy

GoDaddy has 3 plans as well. Plan 1 is economy for $1 a month. It includes 1 website, 100GB of storage, 10 databases, unmetetered bandwith. The next plan is the deluxe plan. It has all the same features but it has unlimited websites, unlimited storage, and 25 databases. The third plan is the ultimate plan. It has all the same features of Deluxe but has increased processing power and unlimited databases.

BlueHost

BlueHost is having a sale right now. They have three plans. Basic, Plus, and choice plus. Basic plan gets you 1 website, 50gb storage, and 1 domain for $2.75. The plus plan is $5.45, it gets you unlimited websites and unlimited website storage. The choice plus plan is on sale for $5.45. It has unlimited everything.
